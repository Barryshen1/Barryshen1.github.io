Hi! I'm an incoming PhD student in the Department of [Computer & Information Science & Engineering](https://www.cise.ufl.edu/) at the [University of Florida](https://www.ufl.edu/), starting in Fall 2026, where I will be advised by Prof. [Zhe Jiang](https://www.jiangteam.org/). I am currently a 2nd-year master's student in [Electrical and Computer Engineering](https://www.ece.uw.edu/) at the [University of Washington](https://www.washington.edu/). Prior to UW, I received my BEng from [Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University](https://www.xjtlu.edu.cn/en/study/departments/school-of-advanced-technology) and the [University of Liverpool](https://www.liverpool.ac.uk/engineering/) in 2024, advised by Prof. [Jieming Ma](https://scholar.xjtlu.edu.cn/en/persons/JiemingMa).

Most recently, I was a research intern at the [Yale NLP Lab](https://nlp.cs.yale.edu/), advised by Prof. [Arman Cohan](https://armancohan.com/) and [Yilun Zhao](https://yilunzhao.github.io/). Previously, I worked as a Research Intern on a collaborative project between the University of Washington and [Lenovo Research](https://research.lenovo.com/webapp/view_English/index.html), advised by Prof. [Radha Poovendran](https://people.ece.uw.edu/radha/) and Dr. [Yan Li](http://www.linkedin.com/in/liyancas).
